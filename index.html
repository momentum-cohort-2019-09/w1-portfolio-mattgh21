Ultimate Frisbee:

Playing

I started playing Ultimate in High School at Carolina Friends School. Since then I played two season of YCC with the Triangle U20 Boys team, Triforce, winning the National Championship in 2014 and taking second in 2015. I recently completed my 5 years of college eligibility playing with UNC Darkside where we won 2 National Championships and made 5 straight semifinal appearances. I captained Darkside for 3 years, and I am now in my second year captaining Ring of Fire, the Elite Men’s Club team from Raleigh. I have also had the fortune of playing for and winning gold with the USA U-24 Mixed National Team in 2018 and 2019 in Germany and Australia, respectively.
